About Us

The Wanderers
Behind the Magic

Hi — we're Journey & Co., a boutique travel design studio founded by travellers, for travellers. We believe the best journeys are the ones that change you.

Since 2016, we've been crafting hand-picked itineraries across 60+ countries — weaving together hidden gems, local encounters, and once-in-a-lifetime experiences that no algorithm can replicate.

We don't sell packages. We listen to your dreams and build the trip around your exact vision, budget, and travel style.
